## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no trial period, no catch. The audit is free because Credex makes money when teams with significant savings choose to buy discounted AI credits through us. If you are already spending optimally, we will tell you that honestly.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in the audit engine is sourced directly from official vendor pricing pages and verified during the week of your audit. Sources are documented in our pricing data file. We do not use AI to guess prices — we hardcode verified numbers.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No. You enter your tools, get your audit, and see your results immediately. We only ask for your email after you have seen the results, if you want a copy sent to your inbox.

**Q: Will Credex try to sell me something?**
A: Only if it makes sense for your situation. If your audit shows less than $100/month in savings, we will tell you that you are spending well and offer to notify you if that changes. We only surface Credex as an option for teams with significant savings opportunities where discounted credits would genuinely help.

**Q: How is this different from just reading the pricing pages myself?**
A: The audit engine does not just show you prices. It evaluates whether your specific plan makes sense for your team size and use case, flags when you are paying for features you do not need, and identifies when a cheaper plan from the same vendor would cover your actual usage. It also surfaces the credits opportunity — most teams do not know discounted AI credits exist.
